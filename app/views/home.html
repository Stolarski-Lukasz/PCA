Please note that this site is still in the development stage. It provides a limited number of options and some of them may not always work properly.

Welcome to Phonetic Corpus of Audiobooks. The corpus is based on a selection of free audiobooks available at librivox.org.

You may look for individual words or phrases using the search panel to the left. Use filtering options or available wildcards if you need to narrow down your search. If a given word or phrase is found in the corpus database, you may listen to the corresponding recordings, download them as mp3 files and see additional statistics on the sources from which they were taken.

Click on the help icon to find out more about the corpus. You may also watch a video tutorial offering a comprehensive introduction to the functionality of the corpus.
